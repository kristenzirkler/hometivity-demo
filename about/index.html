Hometivity, Inc. is a diversified corporation that provides debt reduction and payment processing services to homeowners through financial institutions nationwide. The Early Mortgage Pay-Off Plan (EMPP) is a biweekly mortgage acceleration program offered exclusively by Hometivity, Inc.

Track Record of Success

Based in Scottsdale, Arizona, Hometivity began offering the Early Mortgage Pay-Off Plan in December 1991. We have helped hundreds of thousands of homeowners save significant amounts of mortgage interest and build equity in their homes faster.
