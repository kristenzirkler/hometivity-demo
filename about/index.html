Track Record of Success

Hometivity, Inc. is a diversified corporation that provides debt reduction and payment processing services to homeowners through financial institutions nationwide. The Early Mortgage Pay-Off Plan (EMPP) is a biweekly mortgage acceleration program offered exclusively by Hometivity, Inc. Hometivity, Inc. is based in Scottsdale, Arizona. We have been offering the Early Mortgage Pay-Off Plan since December 1991. We have helped hundreds of thousands of homeowners save significant amounts of mortgage interest and build equity in their homes faster.

Experienced Management

Hometivity is led by experienced executives who have expertise in the areas of banking, financial services, management, administration, customer service, information technology, legal services, marketing and sales.

Clifford BendauPresident and Chief Executive Officer

Cliff has forty years of experience in mortgage banking, mortgage brokerage and bank marketing. In 1992, Cliff conceptualized and founded Hometivity, implementing and administering the company's biweekly mortgage payment program at over forty large and medium-sized banks and financial institutions. Cliff prides himself on a dedication to customer service and assisting people achieving their financial goals.

Serge van NeckVice President, Chief Technology Officer

Serge has over twenty-five years of experience in software systems engineering, financial data processing systems and networking. Serge has worked with Cliff since the company's inception and has substantial knowledge of the banking and financial service industry. Serge administers the EMPP program and works directly with its customers to ensure their satisfaction with the program.

Robert S. RederVice President, Chief Operating Officer, General Counsel

Robert has over ten years of experience working with and representing banks and financial institutions in his private legal practice. Robert brings to Hometivity his substantial knowledge of law, finance and customer service. Along with Cliff and Serge, Robert is responsible for the day-to-day operations of Hometivity and the administration of the Early Mortgage Pay-Off Plan.
